Services

Marchione Automotive is a full-service automotive repair shop located in Plumsteadville, Pennsylvania. We are located between Ace Hardware and Wells Fargo Bank.

Performing all service needs from a basic oil change and tire rotation to more elaborate repairs, we work on a wide range of makes and models both foreign and domestic.

Marchione's is also an authorized inspection station, able to perform Pennsylvania State Inspection and Emission tests on any Pennsylvania registered vehicles.
